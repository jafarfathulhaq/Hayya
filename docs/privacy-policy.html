Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

In short: Hayya is designed to be private by default. Your prayer data stays on your device. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Location data: Hayya requests access to your device location solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Your coordinates are processed on-device using the Adhan prayer time calculation library. We do not transmit your location to any server.

Prayer records: Your prayer check-ins, streaks, and qadha history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to use the Companion feature.

Companion data (optional): If you choose to connect with a prayer companion, limited prayer status data (which prayers are marked as done/missed for today only) is synced via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is only visible to your connected companion.

Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • We do not build user profiles or track behavior across apps
  • We do not collect device identifiers for marketing purposes
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Location: To calculate prayer times based on your geographic position. Processed on-device only.
  • Prayer records: To display your dashboard, streak count, and prayer history. Stored locally on your device.
  • Companion sync: To share today's prayer status with your connected companion via iCloud. Only today's status is shared — never historical data.

4. Data Storage and Security

All prayer data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted SwiftData framework. If you use the Companion feature, synced data is stored in your private iCloud container, protected by Apple's security infrastructure and your Apple ID credentials.

We do not operate any external servers. All data processing happens on your device or within Apple's iCloud ecosystem.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Companion feature: Your today-only prayer status is shared with your connected companion through iCloud. You control this by choosing to connect or disconnect.
  • Apple: Standard App Store purchase data is processed by Apple per their privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your prayer records are stored on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Reset All Data. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

If you use iCloud sync, you can manage your iCloud data through Apple's iCloud settings on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Hayya does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation and does not collect identifying information.

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View all your prayer data within the app
  • Export your prayer history as a CSV file
  • Delete all data from Settings
  • Revoke location permissions from iOS Settings
  • Disconnect from your companion at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
