Prayer times are calculated using established Islamic astronomical calculation methods (via the Adhan library). While we strive for accuracy, prayer times are approximations based on mathematical models. We recommend verifying prayer times with your local mosque or Islamic authority, especially during edge cases (extreme latitudes, seasonal transitions).

Hayya is a personal companion tool and should not be considered an authoritative religious source. Always consult qualified Islamic scholars for religious guidance.
